Egypt

Egyptian food is the delicious result of a combination of Greek, Turkish, Lebanese, Palestinian and Syrian influences along with Egyptian tastes and customs. Fruits and vegetables are seasoned with fresh spices to create simple but hearty dishes - here are some recipes you can try at home, for a little taste of Egypt.

Karkady
Karkady is one of the most popular and unusual drinks that you can find in Egypt. Made from the dried, dark red petals of the Hibiscus flower. It is served in many of the popular cafe's throughout Egypt. The best Karkady comes from Upper Egypt. Be sure to bring home a bag of dried petals from Aswan or Luxor.

UAE

With seven different emirates making up the United Arab Emirates, it does not come as a surprise that there is no no solid national cuisine. Instead, the central location of the emirates in the Middle East means that the dishes cooked by locals tend to be fusions of various Middle Eastern styles, particularly those of Iran and Lebanon. So... what exactly is United Arab Emirates cuisine? Find out for yourself now.
