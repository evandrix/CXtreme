Ingredients:
20 ounce chic peas (Hummus)
1/4 cup lemon juice
1/3 cup Tahini
1/4 cup cold water
2 tablespoon cold water
1 tablespoon olive oil for garnish
parsley chopped, for garnish
paprika for garnish
to taste salt
to taste pepper

Instructions:
Mix all ingredients (except for olive oil, parsley, paprika) in food processor until a smooth paste is formed. Garnish with paprika, parsley and olive oil. Serve with toasted Pita bread. To toast the bread, preheat oven to 350 degrees until golden. Makes 4 servings.
