Micromouse
- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

From last year, CXtreme has embarked on a new project which has attracted a lot of participants. It is the micromouse, a robot costing S$2969. Currently the school has purchased 1 micromouse set. The micromouse competition is open to all JCs, and involves programming at a higher level as coding is done manually in C, where participants define their own functions and routines. The micromouse we use is a souped-up version of the MKIT 900. As our code is getting too complex for the MIGS IDE to handle, we have switched to the IDE2K C development environment. We will be having courses on the C language and Micromouse API.
